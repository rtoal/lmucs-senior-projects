The LMU Computer Science Senior Projects

LMU was one of the first universities to require both individual and group-based semester-long projects for the B.S. degree in Computer Science. Building these projects is always a great deal of fun and a great learning experience, as students nearly always have to learn a bunch of new technologies to complete projects of that scope. In addition, the end-of-term project presentation is one of the most memorable highlights of the undergraduate experience. Some of the presentations are even on Vimeo.

Help us complete our history! If you see any mistakes at all, or if you would like to change your name or your project's name, or you know some missing details, please let us know. You can create an issue or send us a pull request.

And please tell your friends who have lost touch to check out the list, too.
